Intro

This guide will help you to understand the working and programs of Microprocessors and Sensors.

How it Works

The microprocessor follows a sequence: Fetch, Decode, and then Execute. ... The microprocessor fetches those instructions from the memory, then decodes it and executes those instructions till STOP instruction is reached. Later, it sends the result in binary to the output port.
